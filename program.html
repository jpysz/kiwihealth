About the Program

1-on-1 Health Coaching

Everyone has unique health needs and goals, which is why I take an individualized and personalized approach for each client. We do a deep dive into your health history, lifestyle, and eating patterns. As a problem-solver at heart, I take a holistic point of view to uncover the root cause of your health and weight struggles to eliminate them once and for all. We’ll explore your diet, fitness, mental health, relationship to food, and how it’s all interconnected.

This program is not a diet. It’s been proven that diets fail over 80% of the time, which is why I will never promote them or recommend them for my clients1. I help you reach your goals in a sustainable way by shifting your mindset and lifestyle and developing positive, self-sufficient, lifelong habits that you’ll always have in your back pocket.

This program is 100% online over video-chat which means you can do coaching calls with me from the comfort of your own home. We’ll meet once a week for two months, then have intermittent ongoing calls after that for however long you need. Ultimately, my goal is to set you up for lifelong success so that you’ll never need another program or coach again. Each week I send you the materials you’ll need to be successful. I support you every step of the way by answering questions and providing guidance whenever you need it.
