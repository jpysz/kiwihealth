About Me

Jennifer Pysz

I’ve had my fair share of health struggles, so I know what it’s like to feel out of balance and not like yourself. My health decline started in childhood, when I was overprescribed antibiotics for recurring sinus infections which threw my gut microbiome out of whack and caused a cascade of digestion and stomach problems including lactose intolerance, bloating, and small intestinal bacterial overgrowth. As my stomach problems got worse I got out of touch with my body and started a cycle of under-eating then over-eating, but never feeling quite right. The physical stress of my gut issues and the yo-yo eating combined with other life stressors, combined with the stress of my Master’s program, caused me to develop Hashimoto’s thyroiditis, an autoimmune disease. At my worst I was overwhelmed with chronic fatigue, debilitating anxiety, muscle aches, acne, weight gain, and hormonal imbalance.

After going from doctor to doctor and trying every diet in the book, but never getting the healing results I was looking for, I took matters into my own hands and started researching how to get my health and my life back. Over the course of hundreds of hours of research I put together the pieces of the puzzle and started to regain my health and get back to feeling like myself again. My energy started to increase and my weight and bloat started going down. Using a combination of herbal supplements, mindfulness, and various food and lifestyle practices that added more balance into my life, I was able to finally heal.

Now, I’m taking my passion for wellbeing and my health expertise to help people who are struggling with their own health and weight issues to become the most optimal version of themselves!
